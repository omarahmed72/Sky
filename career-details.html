Business Development Consultant (Nawy Partners)

On-site · Nawy Partners · Full time

New Cairo City, Cairo Governorate, Egypt

Description

Share this job

Business Development Job Responsibilities:

  • Identify new and potential partnerships to grow Nawy Partners business line, and engage in networking opportunities.
  • Develop client relationships and strengthen industry partnerships.
  • Negotiate and close business deals that promote sustained revenue.
  • Ability to analyze market trends and competitor behavior.
  • Ability to advise on possible joint ventures, mergers, and acquisition.

Requirements

  • Bachelor's Degree in any field.
  • 2 + years of Sales Experience. B2B Experience or E-commerce background is preferred.
  • Real Estate experience is a plus.
  • Strong Stakeholder Management and Communication Skills
  • Negotiation and Influencing Skills.
