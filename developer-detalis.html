About ARDIC For Real Estate Development

Ardic Development is one of the top-rated real estate development companies in Egypt. The company started in 1998, and since then, it has been playing a pivotal role in shaping the Egyptian real estate landscape.

The ARDIC For Real Estate Development and Investment Company has been in the market for more than 25 years, during which it has developed many landmark projects.

Ardic Development has developed more than 3 successful projects in a series called "Zizinia" with variations in name according to the project.

The company has focused its efforts on East Cairo to develop its landmark projects, varying the locations between New Cairo City and Mostakbal City.

Also, among the very well-known real estate projects by Ardic Development are Zizinia Gardens in New Cairo and Zizinia Al Mostakbal in Mostakbal City.

Ardic Development Vision, Mission & Values

Ardic Development has always been driven by an ambitious vision, a strong mission statement, and a solid value proposition.

The company views itself as one of the leading real estate developers in Egypt. Ardic Development strives to achieve such an ambitious vision by offering high-quality projects to the market.

Moreover, the company's mission is to develop communities that hold value and meaning to its residents and not just mere concrete buildings. This mission statement is being worked on through the integration of innovation in all of its real estate projects.

Moreover, Ardic Development is highly driven by a coherent value proposition. Thanks to following a strict value system with these values at its core:

  • Inspiration
  • Creativity
  • Satisfaction
  • Respect
  • Quality
