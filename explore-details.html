About Stei8ht - New Cairo

Stei8ht by LMD is a prominent gated community developed as a partnership between industry leaders. The compound is unique for its premium location and unparalleled architectural design.

The Stei8ht Compound offers the residents many exclusive experiences, all of which enjoy unobstructed views. The compound is also home to many upscale residential properties as well as premium facilities.

Stei8ht enjoys a prime location in New Cairo, offering easy access to major highways and essential landmarks.

Major Facilities & Services in Stei8ht

Thanks to its partnership with well-known developers, LMD was able to craft a smart master plan for Stei8ht. The land plot spans a massive area designed with sustainability in mind.

The master plan indulges a wide selection of blues and greens. These colors are embodied in the water bodies running through the compound, and the abundant, lush greenery found all over.

Stei8ht features a wide selection of recreational facilities aimed at offering the residents a fun time. The compound also boasts a vast range of social hubs created to give the residents quality time with their loved ones.

Services of Stei8ht include high-end maintenance and cleaning services. Also, there is a top-rated round-the-clock security service that depends on full-coverage CCTV surveillance and well-trained security guards.
